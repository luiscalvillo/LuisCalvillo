# Luis Calvillo 

**`iOS Developer | Co-Founder of WhattaEat | Tech Founder`**

I'm a San Diego-based iOS developer and entrepreneur with a proven track record of building and launching consumer mobile apps.

### Current Project
**[WhattaEat](https://whattaeat.com/)** — A video-first social food discovery app.  

Users discover restaurants, street vendors, food trucks, and hidden gems through authentic short videos and an interactive map filled with food emojis — moving beyond static photos and star ratings.

### Apps & Projects

**[ShredCam](https://shredcam.app/)** — Creator.  

An iOS skate camera app built for skateboarders who film themselves. ShredCam helps skaters record clips, review each take, and instantly decide whether to save or delete footage so they can keep their camera roll clean and stay focused on skating.

**[ShredSpots](https://shredspots.com/)** — Founder, successfully exited.  

A skate spot discovery app that grew to **over 100,000 downloads** and ranked in the Top 200 Sports apps on the App Store before being acquired in 2021.

### Other Work
Creator of **App Canvas** — A framework focused on improving mobile app retention and building products people actually use daily.

Always happy to connect with other developers, founders, and skaters. Feel free to reach out!
